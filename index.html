About

Angelico thrives on his: sense of curiousity, creative thinking, and passion for technology.

During his studies in primary and secondary school, Angelico consistently excelled in classes involving: Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. Angelico then continued his studies at the University of Windsor, and eventually obtained his Honours Degree in Computer Science (with Great Distinction) in May of 2017.

Angelico worked as a Teaching Assistant at the University of Windsor for the following courses: Advanced Website Design, Computer Concepts for End-Users, Introduction to Algorithms and Programming II, and Introduction to the Internet. He also worked (and continues to work) at CIBC Wood Gundy as a part-time Sales Assistant / Programmer.

On his free time, Angelico enjoys working on his personal projects to learn new (or sharpen his existing) technical skills. He also likes to ensure a healthy lifestyle by following a daily workout routine.
